Enzo Ferrari

Enzo Ferrari was the creator of Ferrari. He was an engineer; ever since he was little kid he
always wanted to learn how to make cars. Then he made his first ferrari which sky rocketed
the brand and created of the best supercar brands in the world.

Ferrari 488 Pista

The ferrari 488 Pista is one of Ferrari's most well known cars they've made.
This thing clocks in at 710 horsepower which is a beast by the way and goes from 0 to 60
in 2.8 sec.

LaFerrari

The LaFerrari is my favorite Ferrari they've ever created since it looks a lot different
compared to other ferraris. This is because its more aerodynamic then the other ones.
This car clocks in at 950 horsepower which is a lot. This thing goes from 0 to 60 in 2.1 sec
thats super fast

Ferrari F8 Spider

The Ferrari F8 Spider is one of the supercars used in racing. Its pretty fast clocking in
